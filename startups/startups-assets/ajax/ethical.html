Ethical Electric
Direct-to-consumer renewable energy platform
Ethical Electric offers renewable-energy as a service through a 100% renewable energy premium retail energy product or through a long-term savings community solar subscription product. Tens of millions of electricity customers want renewable energy. But only 13% of customers can get rooftop solar and utilities typically purchase little renewable energy, relying on coal, fracked gas, and nuclear. Ethical Electric makes it easy to either pay a little more and get 100% renewable energy in minutes or with community solar, we can offer the long-term savings of rooftop solar, to 54% of households. All with no home visit, no construction project on your roof, and easy enrollment. This provides an easy choice for those who are not looking for a long-term commitment to go renewable, or for those who want savings can make a long-term commitment and get solar for their residence.

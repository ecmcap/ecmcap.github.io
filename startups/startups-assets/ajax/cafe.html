Cafe X
Robotic Café - Premium Coffee in 10 Seconds
Company Mission The goal of Cafe X is to drive the mass adoption of automation technology in the service industry to increase the overall productivity of humanity. We are demonstrating that it is possible to create a better service experience with a fully automated premium robotic café. Coffee Quality Our coffee quality meets or exceeds the standards of top specialty cafés. We work with illy and local coffee roasters in each market. Arrival Detection Proximity based drink pre-preparation using geofencing and bluetooth beacons. Goal: when you arrive at the Robotic Café, your drink is ready for pickup. Business Model Own and operate with revenue sharing to office buildings, shopping malls, airports, universities, etc.. Launch San Francisco, Miami, Hong Kong mid-2016 Launching via franchise partner in Hangzhou and Shanghai late-2016.

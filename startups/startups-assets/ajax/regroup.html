Regroup Therapy
Simple, secure, virtual mental health teams via video to your health system
Regroup provides virtual behavioral health teams to health entities who want to lower their total cost of care, and improve patient outcomes and satisfaction. This is done using tailored deployments of mental health professionals, cutting edge provision of care, and a delivery platform that brings key data/analytics to stakeholders. The Regroup platform delivers high quality care at a lower cost and is responsive to specific population needs.

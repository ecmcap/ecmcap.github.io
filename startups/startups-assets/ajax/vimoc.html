Powerful distributed computing IoT platform
Landscape Computing IoT Platform is an advanced solution shifting the Internet of Things paradigm from connecting Things to the Understanding of things at scale and with high level of security. Landscape Computing is an advanced enterprise IoT full stack platform. It is an application that provides services and interoperability beyond operating systems to various components and applications of a distributed system. An IoT platform is about enabling connectivity between objects. In a more sophisticated form, Landscape Computing consists of a variety of important building blocks: Connectivity & normalization, device management, database, processing & action management, analytics, visualization, & external interfaces.

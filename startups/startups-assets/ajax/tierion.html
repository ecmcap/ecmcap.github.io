Tierion
Collect & Share Verifiable Data
Tierion lets you create a verifiable record of any data or business process on the Bitcoin blockchain. Our cloud database generates a blockchain receipt for each record. This proof allows anyone to verify the authenticity and integrity of any data, file, or communication. Use cases from early customers include: auditing the usage and maintenance history of industrial medical equipment, automating & tracking healthcare business processes, verifiable customer onboarding, and tracking the provenance of marketing leads. Developers can sign up for our SaaS application and get started in minutes.

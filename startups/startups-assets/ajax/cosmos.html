Cosmos Labs
Make your smartphone plan affordable with microfinance driven mobile data plans.
Cosmos Labs is a Techstars Mobility company that makes smartphone ownership affordable via app-based pricing for the 64 million Americans and 5 billion people worldwide who have yet to experience constant mobile connectivity. This product puts access to entertainment, media, education, community, and finance tools in new hands. With Cosmos, the the ability to purchase "access to an app for the day" (buying internet that supports that app) is baked into the core Android experience.
